# Choose Your Party — Brand Guidelines

## Product Identity

**Choose Your Party is an event discovery platform.** Users discover parties and events that match their vibe. Social connection is a secondary benefit that emerges from event discovery — not the primary pitch.

## Voice & Tone

| We sound like | We don't sound like |
|---|---|
| Honest and direct | Corporate jargon |
| Slightly irreverent | Political language |
| Conversational | Data-heavy analysis |
| Specific, not vague | Artificial urgency |

### Messaging Pillars
1. Authenticity over affiliation
2. Discovery, not prescription
3. Connection through shared experience

### Example Copy (On-Brand)
- "Find parties that actually get you"
- "See what's happening tonight based on what you actually like"
- "Stop scrolling. Start going."

### Example Copy (Off-Brand)
- "Join the movement" — too political
- "Connect with like-minded individuals" — too LinkedIn
- "42k parties indexed" — data over people
- "Limited spots available!" — artificial urgency

## Color Palette

| Token | Value | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| `--navy` | `#1a1a3e` | Primary background, dominant (60–70%) |
| `--cyan` | `#60a5fa` | Interactive elements, links, discovery actions |
| `--amber` | `#f59e0b` | Primary CTAs (Find Events, RSVP), warmth |
| `--white` | `#ffffff` | Text, breathing room, contrast |
| `--alert-red` | `#ef4444` | Errors and alerts only |

**Rule:** Navy dominates. Cyan for interaction. Amber only for primary calls to action. One accent color per view.

## Typography

| Role | Font | Weight |
|---|---|---|
| Headlines | Playfair Display | 700 Bold, 700 Italic |
| Body | DM Sans | 400 Regular, 500 Medium, 600 Semibold |

Headlines carry warmth and character. Body text prioritizes readability.

## Photography Direction

- Candid party, concert, venue, people-at-events photography
- Real people in real moments — not posed, not abstract
- High energy but not aggressive
- Warm lighting preferred (amber/gold tones complement brand)

**Never use:** Data visualizations, maps, abstract connection imagery, generic stock "business" photography, political imagery.

## CTAs

- **Primary (amber):** "Discover Events," "Find Events Now," "Find My Night"
- **Secondary (cyan):** "Take the Quiz," "See What's On," "Explore Events"
- **Avoid:** "Sign Up," "Register," "Join Now" — sounds like obligation not discovery
